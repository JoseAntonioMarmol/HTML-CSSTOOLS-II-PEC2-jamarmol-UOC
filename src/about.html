Join us and be part of a unique culinary competition!

The Enchanting Kitchen Contest is an exciting event that gathers cooking enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels. We strive to foster camaraderie, fair play, and fun in the art of cooking.

Some of the scheduled activities include:

  1. Contests
    Participate in our contests to challenge your skills and enjoy exciting cooking battles.
  2. Courses and Workshops
    We offer courses and workshops to enhance your culinary skills under the guidance of expert chefs.
  3. Culinary Workshops
    Engage in hands-on culinary workshops where you can learn new techniques and recipes.
  4. Extra Activities
    Beyond the kitchen, we organize dinners, hiking trips, and other activities to enhance your experience during the contest.
We look forward to seeing you at the contest!

At the Enchanting Kitchen Contest, we strive to create an atmosphere filled with excitement and passion for cooking. Our main goal is to inspire participants of all ages and skill levels to showcase their talent, connect with fellow cooking enthusiasts, and enjoy the thrill of competition.

Whether you're an experienced chef or taking your first steps in cooking, everyone is welcome to participate in the contest! Feel free to come and ask our staff
